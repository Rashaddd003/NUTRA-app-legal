Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information NUTRA collects, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use NUTRA, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (managed securely via Supabase Authentication).
  • Profile Information: Display name, date of birth, sex, height, weight, activity level, dietary patterns, and food allergies.
  • Health & Wellness Data: Daily logs including calories consumed, sleep hours, water intake, steps, energy and hunger scores, and adherence tracking.
  • Questionnaire Responses: Answers you provide during onboarding and follow-up questionnaires to personalise your experience.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App (e.g., features used, session duration) to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalised AI-powered nutrition plans, workout suggestions, and supplement protocols.
  • Track your daily progress and calculate adherence scores.
  • Improve and optimise the App experience.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. AI Processing

Your profile and health data may be processed by third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI) to generate personalised plans. This data is sent securely via our backend and is not used by AI providers to train their models. We do not sell your data.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (backed by PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security (RLS) enabled, meaning only you can access your own data. All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With AI processing partners (as described above) solely for generating your plans.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (via the Settings screen), all associated personal data is permanently removed from our database.
